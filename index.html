Margin and Padding

Grey Area: This shows a margin of 20px.

Yellow Area: This shows 20px padding.

This displays how Margin, Padding and Borders affect the layout of html elements. Margin (margin:) effects the space outside of an element and decides how far away the element moves from the closest elements. Padding (padding:) affects the space inside the element and controls how close things can come to the inside edge. A border (border:) will affect the size of the element as the border width will be included in the element's overall height and width.
